Hi! We’re Lidia and Andrei, a couple who turned traveling and filmmaking into our lifestyle. For the past four years, we’ve been living abroad, exploring new places, and capturing them on camera. We film together, taking turns behind the lens and adding our own details to make every story unique.

Lidia brings the magic in post-production: editing, color grading, sound design, and music.

With her modeling background, she also knows how to shine in front of the camera.

Andrei is the diamond cut of our projects, the one who shapes ideas and polishes every detail until it shines. He sparks concepts, draws storyboards, and makes sure each video feels alive.

Together, we create videos that aren’t just about destinations,
they’re about the feeling of being there.
